# Overview of FMs on Amazon Bedrock

Foundation models (FMs) are large-scale AI models that have been trained on vast amounts of data and can perform a variety of tasks such as text generation, summarization, image creation, video generation, and more. Amazon Bedrock provides access to these models, allowing everyone to use powerful AI capabilities without needing to manage infrastructure.

Some capabilities of Amazon Bedrock foundation models include:

- **Text generation**: Creating human-like text based on input prompts.
    
- **Text embeddings**: Converting text into numerical representations for tasks like semantic search.
    
- **Question answering**: Extracting answers from models or knowledge bases based on user queries.
    
- **Summarization**: Condensing large amounts of text into key insights.
    
- **Translation**: Converting text from one language to another.
    
- **Image and video generation**: Creating images or videos based on textual descriptions.
    
- **Chat support**: Creating conversational agents to interact with user
