This note series is a **day-wise learning journey on Database Modeling**, covering:

- Core **concepts and theories**
- Essential **terminologies**
- **Small hands-on exercises** to build intuition
- Practical thinking for **real-world database design**

The goal is to **build or refresh strong fundamentals** in database modeling in a structured, easy-to-follow way.  
Each day focuses on **one clear concept**, making it ideal for consistent daily learning.

## Who Is This Note Series For?

This series is useful for:

- **Data Engineers / AI Engineers** who want to _revise rusty concepts_
- **Beginners** who want to learn database modeling from scratch
- Learners who enjoy **learning one small concept every day**
- Anyone aiming to **complete a monthly learning checklist**
- Professionals who want to **translate theory into practical modeling skills**

In short:  
If you want clarity, consistency, and confidence in database modeling, this series is for you.

By the end of this series, you will gain:
1. Strong understanding of core database design principles

3. Hands-on experience with data modeling techniques
4. Awareness of performance-oriented and scalable database design
